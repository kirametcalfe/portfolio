bio

Kira Metcalfe, a self-described nerd, loves everything fandom and regularly writes about entertainment and pop culture. Kira graduated from Toronto Metropolitan University in 2016 with BA in English Literature. She has poetry and prose published in a variety of online and print media, including the short story The Golden Statue. Kira is currently working on her first novel, an occult thriller.
